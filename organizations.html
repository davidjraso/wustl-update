Association of Latin American Students

ALAS is the premier cultural group for students of Latin American descent at Wash U.

Bhangra Team

The Bhangra Team is one of the Indian dance groups on campus. They are directed by Ashoka, the South East Asian student group.

Campus Kitchen

Campus Kitchen is a volunteer student organization that aims to address food insecurity and waste.

Campus Y

Campus Y allows students to grow as leaders in programs run through the YMCA.

Catholic Student Center

The Catholic Student Center, run by Father Gary, is a non-denominational resource for all christian students.

Leaders in Interpersonal Violence Education

Live is an organization which aims to hold facilitations to promote awareness and prevention of sexual violence.

Mama's Pot Roast

Mama's Pot Roast is an improv/comedy group that holds various sketch shows throughout the year.

Social Programming Board

SPB is one of the largest student organization which puts on comedy shows, musical performances, and much more throughout the year.

Student Union

Student Union is the government on campus which allocates budgets to more than 200 student groups and puts on a variety of events.

Synapse

Synapse is a student organization focusing on neuroscience related activities both on campus and in the St. Louis community.

WU Churn

WU Churn is the premier buttern churning club on campus.
