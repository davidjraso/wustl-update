Assembly Series: “An Evening in Eddie’s World”

Monday, April 11, 2016 • 7:00 pm

Eddie Huang, a bright, brash, and hardworking Taiwanese kid who never let race define who he was or who he could be. NOTE: This program will have restricted access for the public. Doors open at 6 p.m. Co-sponsored by SU Speakers Series and Chinese Students Assoc. Graham Chapel.

DUC – Tuesday Tea @ 3

Tuesday, April 12, 2016 • 3:00 pm

Every Tuesday that school is in session – free hot tea and treats. Hosted this week by Marrow Registry. DUC, 1st Fl., Tisch Commons.

ThurtenE Carnival 2016

Friday, April 15, 2016 • 4:00 pm

The purpose of the carnival is to bring together the WashU and greater St. Louis communities for three days of exhilarating rides, live performances, and delicious food. Organized by members of ThurtenE Honorary. Ready Readers St. Louis is ThurtenE’s community partner for 2016. Location: in front of Brookings Hall on the WashU campus.
